Chess

Pick white piece to move


Rules

This game follows the rule of Chess with the following exceptions:

  • Pawns cannot execute en passant.
  • Pawns are promoted to queen.
  • Castling is not available.

In order to play this game, follow the instructions on the top of this board. The player's turn is the turn listed above. In order to select a piece, click on the piece you want to move. Then move it into a valid position.
